Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2025

DSOFT SOLUTIONS (“us”, “we”, or “our”) operates the DSOFT POS mobile application and website. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, and Google profile data when logging in via Google Sign-In.
  • Business Data: Menu setup, order history, and table management info saved by you in the app.
  • Firebase Data: User interactions, business activity, and app behavior stored in Firebase Database and Cloud Storage.
  • Analytics Data: Device type, OS version, app version, error logs, crash reports, and anonymized usage trends.
  • Location Data: (only if explicitly used or required by a specific feature — e.g., printing over LAN/WiFi).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate and authorize access to your account
  • To store and retrieve your POS data (menus, sales, orders)
  • To export data to your connected Google Sheets account
  • To analyze app performance and improve stability
  • To respond to customer inquiries and provide technical support

3. Third-Party Services We Use

  • Firebase Authentication – For secure user login
  • Firebase Realtime Database / Firestore – To store business data
  • Firebase Cloud Storage – To save images, receipts, or logos
  • Google Sign-In – For quick authentication
  • Google Sheets API & Drive API – For exporting business data
  • Google Analytics (via Firebase) – To understand usage patterns
